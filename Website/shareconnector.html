Why Choose ShareConnector?

The intelligent way to share media across all your services

Universal Sharing

Share any media link from any Android application directly to ShareConnector. No more copying URLs or switching between apps - just tap share and let intelligent routing handle the rest.

Smart Content Recognition

AI-powered content recognition automatically identifies media types and routes them to the appropriate connector. YouTube videos go to download managers, torrent links to torrent clients, and more.

20+ Intelligent Connectors

Connect to torrent clients, media servers, download managers, cloud storage, and specialized services. Each connector is designed for optimal performance and seamless integration.

Secure & Private

Your data stays on your devices. ShareConnector uses secure connections and encrypted storage for all your service credentials and configuration data.

Real-time Synchronization

Stay connected with real-time sync across all your ShareConnect applications. Changes in one app are instantly reflected across your entire ecosystem.

Modern Android Experience

Built with modern Android architecture using Kotlin, Material Design 3, and the latest Android APIs. Enjoy a smooth, responsive experience on all your devices.

Experience seamless media sharing in three simple steps

1

Find Your Media

Discover media content from any app - YouTube, Twitter, Reddit, news sites, or any other source that shares links.

Tap Share

Use the native Android share menu from any app. Select ShareConnector from the share options.

3

Automatic Routing

ShareConnector automatically recognizes the content type and routes it to your configured services. Downloads start immediately.
